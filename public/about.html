About Us

We are the Pleasant Pantry Professionals. We are a group of 4 Junior Norhwestern Web Developers. With our combine skillset, our application was built using Materialize CSS and Edamam API. With this app we wanted to allow users to create a shopping list based on a recipe that they searched. They can add and remove items from a grocery list to allow them to customize their personal shopping items for a delicious home made meal experience. Happy Eating!
